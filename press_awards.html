Awards

Featured in the 2018 Youth Solutions Report

The Youth Solutions Report, a flagship initiative of the Sustainable Development Solutions Network - Youth, features 50 game-changing projects led by young people, allowing them to showcase their work, and presenting them with opportunities to draw interest from potential supporters.

1st prize recipient - Lavazza and Youth for SDGs

HowtUyoga won the first prize in the Lavazza and Youth for SDGs national contest, issued by the Lavazza Foundation. The goal of the contest was to propose an innovative project in the field of sustainable development, with particular emphasis on coffee cultivation, and it was open to all Italian universities. The prize has been awarded to us by Marco Lavazza, vice-president of the Lavazza Group, in the presence of Valeria Fedeli, the then Minister of Education.
